## Systemic Issues (apply everywhere)

### Issue 1: Em Dashes — PERVASIVE
Found in **30+ locations** across site and emails. User's #1 instant-fail AI tell.
- Every FAQ answer uses em dashes
- Origin story, product intro body, PDP description, trust signals
- All 5 welcome emails contain em dashes
- Even alt text on images uses em dashes

### Issue 2: Tricolon Repetition ("No X. No Y. No Z.") — PERVASIVE
Found in **10+ locations**. User's #2 instant-fail AI tell.
- Hero subhead: "No pills. No planning. No compromise."
- Buy box: "No pills, no fillers, no planning. Just sprinkle and enjoy dairy again."
- Origin story: "No preservatives. No fillers. No compromise."
- FAQ 7: "No preservatives, no fillers, no additives, no artificial flavors."
- PDP trust 4: "No preservatives, no fillers, no additives"
- PDP ingredients: "No preservatives, no fillers, no additives, no artificial flavors."
- FAQ 12: "No gluten, no dairy, no soy, no nuts, no common allergens."
- Email 1 How It Works: "No timing. No guessing. No swallowing..."
- Email 4 reveal: "No fillers. No binders. No 'natural flavors.'"

### Issue 3: Sentence Length Uniformity
FAQ answers are especially formulaic. Most sentences hover at 15-20 words with no fragments, no run-ons, no rhythm breaks. The cadence is metronomic.
